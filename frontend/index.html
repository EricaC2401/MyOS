Data caveats & calculation notes

All percentages on the dashboard are calculated as a percentage of income.

Row 1 — Income & accrual view

MetricHow it is calculated
IncomeSum of all income transactions in the selected period.
Allowable ExpensesSum of expenses marked as tax-deductible in the selected period.
Tax LiabilityEstimated tax burden for the period. Monthly view: total tax due entries in the relevant financial year ÷ 12. Financial year view: total tax due entries for that year. Other views: actual tax payments (expenses in the "Tax Payment" group).
Accrued ExpensesTotal expenses (excl. Tax Payment group) with annual costs (e.g. Car Related: Annual) spread monthly (÷ 12) instead of showing the full amount in the month paid.
Saving (Used)Income − Accrued Expenses − Tax Liability. Represents smoothed saving after spreading annual costs.

Row 2 — Cash basis view

MetricHow it is calculated
Allocated Tax PaidTotal tax payment expenses in the relevant financial year ÷ 12. Spreads the actual tax paid across months evenly. Subtitle shows actual tax payment expenses recorded in the selected period.
Expenses PaidTotal expenses (excl. Tax Payment group) as actually paid in the period. Full amounts shown in the month they occurred.
Cash OutflowExpenses Paid + actual tax paid this period. Total cash leaving your accounts in the period.
Saving (Paid)Income − Expenses Paid − Tax Liability. Cash-basis saving for the period.
Net Cash FlowCash inflow (income received) − cash outflow (expenses + tax payments) for the period.

Key differences: Row 1 vs Row 2

ConceptRow 1 (Accrual)Row 2 (Cash)
Annual expensesSpread monthly (÷ 12)Full amount in month paid
TaxTax Liability from tax due entriesAllocated Tax Paid from actual payments
Best forUnderstanding true monthly cost of livingTracking actual cash movements

Expense breakout

Expenses are classified into super-groups (Housing, Driving Related, Necessaries, etc.) based on mappings configured in Settings → Expense Classifications. The "Paid" view shows actual amounts; the "Used" view distributes the used/paid adjustment proportionally across non-tax classifications.

Financial year

UK tax year: 6 April to 5 April. When viewing by month, Tax Liability and Allocated Tax Paid reference the financial year containing that month and divide by 12.

HKD conversion

HKD-only expenses are converted to GBP using HMRC monthly exchange rates where available.
